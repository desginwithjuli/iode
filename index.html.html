About IODE

The Institute of Open and Distance Education (IODE) is a Delhi-based education platform dedicated to providing accessible and affordable education through distance mode. We empower students—especially those from rural and working backgrounds—to pursue bachelor's and master's degrees in a flexible format.

Our Vision

"To become India's leading platform for inclusive and accessible distance education, nurturing knowledge and skill in every corner of the country."

Our Mission

  • Deliver quality education in a flexible mode
  • Digitally support students with modern tools
  • Offer career-relevant UG/PG programs
  • Bridge the gap between traditional learning and modern needs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs, admission process, and more.

You can apply for admission by visiting our website and filling out the online application form. After submitting the form, you'll need to upload the required documents and pay the application fee. Once your application is processed, you'll receive an enrollment confirmation.

Yes, all our programs are recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and are valid for higher education and employment purposes throughout India and abroad.

After enrollment, you can access all study materials through our student portal. We provide digital PDFs of course materials that you can download and study at your convenience. Physical copies can also be requested for an additional fee.

We follow a semester-based examination system. Each semester has end-term examinations, and some courses may include assignments and practical assessments. The examination schedule is published in the academic calendar, and admit cards are issued before exams.

Yes, we do accept credit transfers from recognized universities subject to our academic committee's approval. You'll need to submit your previous marksheets and course details during the application process for evaluation.
